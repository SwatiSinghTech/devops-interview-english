## Kyun Sirf 3 Tenses

English grammar ki poori book bhul jao. **Sirf 3 tenses** master karo — yahi interview mein sabse zyada use hote hain.

| Tense | Use For |
|---|---|
| **Present Continuous** | Jo **abhi** ho raha hai |
| **Present Indefinite** | Jo **daily / routine** mein hota hai |
| **Present Perfect Continuous** | Jo **X time se** chal raha hai |

---

## 1. Present Continuous

### Kab Use Karein

Jab aap kuch **abhi** kar rahe ho — currently, nowadays, in office.

### Formula

> **Subject + is / am / are + Verb-ing + Object**

### Examples (Hindi to English)

| Hindi | English |
|---|---|
| Main DevOps mein kaam kar raha hu | **I am working** in DevOps |
| Hum Azure use kar rahe hain | **We are using** Azure |
| Pipeline build ho rahi hai | The pipeline **is building** |
| Main Terraform seekh raha hu | **I am learning** Terraform |

### Interview Tip

Apna **current work** batane ke liye yahi tense use hota hai. *"Currently, I am working on..."*

---

## 2. Present Indefinite (Simple Present)

### Kab Use Karein

Jab aap **routine**, **responsibility**, ya **fact** bata rahe ho.

### Formula

> **Subject + Verb (1st form) + Object**
>
> Yahan **is / am / are NAHI** lagta!

### Examples (Hindi to English)

| Hindi | English |
|---|---|
| Main pipelines handle karta hu | **I handle** pipelines |
| Hum Terraform use karte hain | **We use** Terraform |
| Yeh tool fast deploy karta hai | This tool **deploys** fast |
| Team daily standup karti hai | The team **does** a daily standup |

### Common Mistake — Dhyan Do

**WRONG:** *"I am work in DevOps"*
**RIGHT:** *"I work in DevOps"*

**WRONG:** *"I am handle pipelines"*
**RIGHT:** *"I handle pipelines"*

---

## 3. Present Perfect Continuous

### Kab Use Karein

Jab batana ho **"X time se kaam kar raha hu"** — **experience batate waqt yeh sabse important hai!**

### Formula

> **Subject + has / have + been + Verb-ing + for / since + time**

- **I / We / You / They** → **have been**
- **He / She / It** → **has been**

### Examples (Hindi to English)

| Hindi | English |
|---|---|
| Main 2 saal se DevOps kar raha hu | **I have been working** in DevOps **for** 2 years |
| Hum 6 mahine se project kar rahe hain | **We have been working for** 6 months |
| Main 2022 se Azure use kar raha hu | **I have been using** Azure **since** 2022 |

### FOR vs SINCE — Yeh Confuse Karta Hai

| FOR | SINCE |
|---|---|
| **Duration** (kitna time) | **Starting point** (kab se) |
| for 2 years | since 2022 |
| for 6 months | since January |

**Trick:** *"FOR = duration ka number, SINCE = specific point"*

## Quick Comparison

| Situation | Tense | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Abhi kar raha hu** | Present Continuous | I **am working** on a project |
| **Daily karta hu** | Present Indefinite | I **work** on Azure |
| **X time se kar raha hu** | Present Perfect Continuous | I **have been working** for 2 years |

---

## Real Interview Example — Teeno Tense Mix

> *"Hi, I am Rahul. **I have been working** as a DevOps Engineer for 2 years. Currently, **I am working** with a client in the fintech domain. **I handle** the infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines."*

Dekho — ek hi paragraph mein teeno tense aaye. Yahi practical English hai.
