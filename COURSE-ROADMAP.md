---

## Daily Practice Routine

Har din yeh karo, bas 30 minutes:

1. **5 min** — Aaj ka lesson topic padho
2. **10 min** — Diagram dekho aur samjho
3. **10 min** — Bol-bol ke practice karo (mirror ya phone)
4. **5 min** — Apna recording sun ke khud judge karo

Bas itna hi. 30 din mein automatic English aane lagega.

---

## Sabse Important Rule

**Ratna nahi hai. Samajhna hai. Bolna hai.**

Rata hua intro robotic lagta hai. Interviewer turant pakad leta hai. Templates ko structure ki tarah lo, script ki tarah nahi.
